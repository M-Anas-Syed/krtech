Based in Dubai, UAE

Khair Rafiq Technical Services LLC (KR Tech) specialises in multiple technical projects of maintenance for interiors and exteriors. Our professional teams provide technical and non-technical services in different fields. These dedicated teams are a mix of multitalented and multi-skilled experienced professionals. These teams do their best in accomplishing their tasks to assure customer satisfaction. Check out some of the services that we provide.

Civil


Our civil construction services include masonry, tiling, typsum partioning and ceiling, interlocks, marble fixing and polishing, wallpaper fixing, aluminium and glass fit-outs etc.

Electrical


Our electrical services include low voltage power, control paneling, general lighting, ELV systems, CCTV, access control, fire alarms and all general electrical maintenance & installation.

Mechanical


Our mechanical services include air-conditioning systems maintenance & installation, duct cleaning & maintenance, plumbing, sanitary & drainage.

Interiors / Exteriors


Our services also include maintenance & repair, carpentry, spray & roller painting, furniture polishing, flooring.
